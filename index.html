Faridabad Model School (FMS) is a school in Faridabad, Haryana, India. The school is located in Sector-31, Faridabad adjacent to National Highway 2. Faridabad Model School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi up to Class XII.

About


Welcome to the world of FMS where we "Inspire young minds into future leaders." Faridabad Model School is ideally located in Sector-31, Faridabad adjacent to National Highway -2 (Delhi- Mathura Road). FMS Sector-31 is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi upto Class XII. Our excellent infrastructure, eco-friendly, spacious and caring environment, highly experienced and committed staff, well balanced and child centric curriculum stimulates creativity and innovation in our children. We nurture creative individualism with a sense of community responsibility within an ethical framework.
